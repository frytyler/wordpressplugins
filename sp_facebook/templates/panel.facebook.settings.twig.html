To set up your Facebook Feed widget, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the username of the Facebook page.
  2. Attain an App ID and Secret Key and copy and paste those in the appropriate fields below. You can do so by going to your Facebook Developer Apps page. Additional info can be found here.
  3. Save those settings by clicking on the "Update Facebook Account" button.
  4. Go to your Widgets section and drag and drop the Facebook Feed widget into your sidebar.
#}
